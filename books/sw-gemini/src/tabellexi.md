<h2>Viwakilishi Vishirikishi Bainishi</h2>
<p>Maneno ya viwakilishi vishirikishi <strong>ke</strong>, <strong>hin</strong>, <strong>den</strong>,
	<strong>ban</strong>, <strong>moy</strong>, <strong>nil</strong>, <strong>alo</strong> na <strong>sama</strong>
	lazima yafuatwe na <em>nomino</em> (iwe imerekebishwa na vivumishi au la) au <em>kiwakilishi</em>. Hayapaswi
	kusimama peke yake kwa sababu kwa kuacha <em>(ki)nomino</em> kiwakilishi kinaweza kuchukuliwa kimakosa kama
	kibainishi cha nomino/kitenzi kinachofuata. Bila nomino maalum, viwakilishi <strong>te</strong> au
	<strong>to</strong> huashiria mwisho wa kirai nomino. Tazama <a
		href="./jumlemonli-estrutur.html#pornamelexi_in_namelexili_jumlemon">Virai Nomino</a>.</p>
<p>Linganisha sentensi zifuatazo:</p>
<p><strong>Hinto bon nasacu.</strong><br /> Hiki (kitu) kinanukia vizuri.</p>
<p>Katika sentensi hapo juu, <strong>-to</strong> inaashiria mwisho wa kirai nomino.</p>
<p><strong>Hin bon nasacu... memorigi mi cel misu femgami.</strong><br /> Hii harufu nzuri... inanikumbusha mke wangu.
</p>
<p>Katika sentensi hapo juu, <strong>nasacu</strong> inaashiria mwisho wa kirai nomino.</p>
<h2><em>kekwanti</em>, <em>kenumer</em></h2>
<p>Vile vile, <strong>kekwanti</strong> (kiasi gani <em>cha</em>) na <strong>kenumer</strong> (idadi gani <em>ya</em>)
	lazima pia zifuatwe na <strong>te</strong> au <strong>to</strong> wakati nomino inaeleweka na haijatajwa.</p>
<p><strong>kenumer bon lala</strong> - nyimbo nzuri ngapi<br /> dhidi ya<br />
	<strong>Kenumer te bon lala?</strong><br /> Wangapi (kati yao) wanaimba vizuri?
</p>
<p><strong>Mi le kari dua kilogramo fe risi. Yu le kari kekwanti to?</strong><br /> Nilinunua kilo mbili za mchele. Wewe
	ulinunua kiasi gani?</p>
<h2><em>cel</em> ya Lazima</h2>
<p>Kihusishi <strong>cel</strong> ni cha lazima pamoja na viwakilishi vishirikishi vya <strong>loka</strong> wakati
	mwendo unahusika. </p>
<p><strong>cel keloka</strong> - wapi (kwenda)<br />
	<strong>cel hinloka</strong> - hapa (<em>kuja</em>)<br />
	<strong>cel denloka</strong> - pale (<em>kwenda</em>)<br /> nk.
</p>
<h2>Maswali dhidi ya Vishazi Viulizi ndani ya Sentensi Taarifa</h2>
<p><em>Vishazi viulizi ndani ya sentensi taarifa</em> ni vishazi ambavyo vinaonekana mahali pa virai nomino na ambavyo
	vinamaanisha <em>jibu la swali "XYZ?"</em> au tofauti yake. Vinaundwa kwa kuvitambulisha kwa kiunganishi cha kishazi
	<strong>ku</strong>, kwa kutumia kibainishi kile kile (<strong>ke</strong>) kinachoonekana katika maswali halisi, na
	kuhifadhi mpangilio wa maneno wa swali husika la kishazi. </p>
<p>Jozi zifuatazo za sentensi za mfano zinaonyesha: (1) maswali halisi, (2) sentensi taarifa zenye vishazi viulizi</p>
<h3>ke - <em>ipi, zipi</em>; kete - <em>nani</em>; keto - <em>nini</em></h3>
<p>(1) <strong>Kete lubi yu?</strong><br /> "Nani anakupenda?"<br /> Nani anakupenda?</p>
<p>(2) <strong>Mi jixi ku kete lubi yu.</strong><br /> "Najua hili: <em>Nani anakupenda?</em>."<br /> Najua nani
	anakupenda. </p>
<p>(1) <strong>Yu lubi kete?</strong><br /> "Unampenda nani."<br /> Unampenda nani?</p>
<p>(2) <strong>Mi jixi ku yu lubi kete.</strong><br /> "Najua hili: <em>Unampenda nani?</em>."<br /> Najua unampenda
	nani. </p>
<p>(1) <strong>Te vole na yam keto?</strong><br /> "Anataka kula nini?"<br /> Anataka kula nini?</p>
<p>(2) <strong>Mi le wanji ku te vole na yam keto.</strong><br /> "Nimesahau hili: <em>Anataka kula nini?</em>."<br />
	Nimesahau anataka kula nini. </p>
<p>(1) <strong>Te le gibe pesa tas ke doste?</strong><br /> "Alimpa pesa rafiki yupi?" Alimpa pesa rafiki yupi? </p>
<p>(2) <strong>Te le no loga ku te le gibe pesa tas ke doste.</strong><br /> "Hakusema hili <em>Alimpa pesa rafiki
		yupi?</em>."<br /> Hakusema alimpa pesa rafiki yupi. </p>
<h3>kesu - <em>-a nani</em></h3>
<p>(1) <strong>Hinto sen kesu kursi?</strong><br /> "Hiki ni kiti cha nani?"<br /> Hiki ni kiti cha nani?</p>
<p>(2) <strong>Mi vole na jixi ku hinto sen kesu kursi.</strong><br /> "Nataka kujua hili: <em>Hiki ni kiti cha
		nani?</em>."<br /> Nataka kujua hiki ni kiti cha nani. </p>
<p>(1) <strong>Kesu kitabu sen per mesa?</strong><br /> "Kitabu cha nani kiko mezani?"<br /> Kitabu cha nani kiko
	mezani.</p>
<p>(2) <strong>Mi jixi ku kesu kitabu sen per mesa.</strong><br /> "Najua hili: <em>Kitabu cha nani kiko
		mezani?</em>."<br /> Najua kitabu cha nani kiko mezani. </p>
<h3>kepul - <em>kama nini au aina gani ya (pamoja na nomino); vipi (pamoja na vitenzi)</em></h3>
<p>(1) <strong>Yu sen kepul?</strong><br /> "Uko vipi?"<br /> U hali gani?</p>
<p>(2) <strong>Te le swal ku yu sen kepul.</strong><br /> "Aliuliza hili: <em>Uko vipi?</em>."<br /> Aliuliza ulikuwa
	hali gani. </p>
<p>(1) <strong>Yu sen kepul insan?</strong><br /> "Wewe ni mtu wa aina gani?"<br /> Wewe ni mtu wa aina gani? </p>
<p>(2) <strong>Mi jixi ku yu sen kepul insan.</strong><br /> "Najua hili: <em>Wewe ni mtu wa aina gani?</em>."<br />
	Najua wewe ni mtu wa aina gani. </p>
<h3>kemo - <em>kiasi gani (kwa kiwango gani)</em></h3>
<p>(1) <strong>Te sen kemo lao?</strong><br /> "Ana umri gani?"<br /> Ana umri gani?</p>
<p>(2) <strong>Te le loga tas mi ku te sen kemo lao.</strong><br /> "Aliniambia hili: <em>Ana umri gani?</em>."<br />
	Aliniambia ana umri gani.</p>
<p>(1) <strong>Yu sen kemo pilodo?</strong><br /> "Umechoka kiasi gani?"<br /> Umechoka kiasi gani?</p>
<p>(2) <strong>Mi jixi ku yu sen kemo pilodo.</strong><br /> Najua hili: "Umechoka kiasi gani?".<br /> Najua umechoka
	kiasi gani. </p>
<h3>kekwanti - <em>kiasi gani</em>; kenumer - <em>ngapi</em></h3>

</p>

<p>(1) <strong>Yu hare kenumer bete?</strong><br /> "Una watoto wangapi?"<br /> Una watoto wangapi? </p>
<p>(2) <strong>Mi jixivole ku yu hare kenumer bete.</strong><br /> "Nashangaa hili: <em>Una watoto wangapi?</em>."<br />
	Nashangaa una watoto wangapi. </p>

<h2>Sentensi Taarifa zenye Virai Viulizi</h2>
<p>Wazungumzaji wakati mwingine watapunguza kishazi kiulizi kuwa kirai, hata kwa neno moja tu la kuuliza. The
	kiunganishi <strong>ku</strong> bado kinatumika bila kishazi kamili.</p>
<p><strong>Mi jixi ku fe ke mesi.</strong><br /> Najua ni katika mwezi gani.</p>
<p><strong>Te le no loga ku keseba.</strong><br /> Hakusema kwa nini.</p>
<p><strong>Dento sen ku keloka.</strong><br /> Hapo ndipo. </p>
<p><strong>Mi jixi ku na idi keloka.</strong><br /> Najua pa kwenda.</p>
<h2>Viunganishi Vishirikishi vya Kirai</h2>
<p>Viunganishi vishirikishi vya kirai huishia na <strong>-loka</strong>, <strong>-watu</strong>, <strong>-seba</strong>
	na <strong>-maner</strong>, na hutumia kiunganishi husishi <strong>hu</strong>. </p>
<p><strong>Mi ergo <em>denloka hu yu ergo</em>.</strong><br /> Ninafanya kazi <em>pale unapofanya kazi</em>.</p>
<p><strong>Mi xa preata <em>denwatu hu yam sen jumbi</em>.</strong> <em>au</em> <strong><em>Denwatu hu yam sen
			jumbi</em>, mi xa preata.</strong><br /> Nitafika <em>wakati chakula kitakapokuwa tayari</em>. <em>au</em>
	<em>Wakati chakula kitakapokuwa tayari</em>, nitafika.
</p>
<p><strong>Mi le no idi cel parti <em>denseba hu yu idi</em>.</strong><br /> Sikuenda kwenye sherehe <em>kwa sababu
		ulienda</em>.</p>
<p><strong>Mi le xuli mobil <em>denmaner hu yu le alim tas mi</em>.</strong><br /> Nilitengeneza gari <em>kama/vile
		ulivyonifundisha</em>.</p>
<p><strong><em>Denmaner hu mi le loga...</em></strong><br />
	<em>Kama nilivyosema</em>...
</p>
<h2>Viwakilishi Vishirikishi vya Ulinganisho</h2>
<p>Kiunganishi <strong>kom</strong> kinamaanisha <em>kama, vile</em> na kinatumika na viwakilishi vishirikishi
	vinavyoishia na <strong>-pul</strong>, <strong>-mo</strong>, <strong>-kwanti</strong> na <strong>-numer</strong>
	kufanya ulinganisho. Katika jozi zifuatazo za sentensi za mfano, sentensi ya pili inachukua nafasi ya neno au kirai
	maalum kwa kiwakilishi kishirikishi. </p>
<p>(1) <strong>Mi sen <em>hazuni kom yu</em>.</strong><br /> Nina <em>huzuni kama wewe</em>. </p>
<p>(2) <strong>Mi sen <em>denpul kom yu</em>.</strong><br /> Mimi ni <em>kama wewe</em>. </p>
<p>(1) <strong>Mi salom yu <em>sodarsim kom misu sodar</em>.</strong><br /> Ninakusalimu <em>kwa udugu kama kaka
		yangu</em>.</p>
<p>(2) <strong>Mi salom yu <em>denpul kom misu sodar</em>.</strong><br /> Ninakusalimu <em>kama kaka yangu</em>.</p>
<p>(1) <strong>Mi no abil na lala <em>meli kom yu</em>.</strong><br /> Siwezi kuimba <em>vizuri kama wewe</em>.</p>
<p>(2) <strong>Mi no abil na lala <em>denpul kom yu</em>.</strong><br /> Siwezi kuimba <em>kama wewe</em>.</p>
<p>(1) <strong><em>Sama kom mi,</em> pia te hare tiga bete.</strong><br />
	<em>Sawa na mimi,</em> yeye pia ana watoto watatu.
</p>
<p>(2) <strong><em>Denpul kom mi,</em> pia te hare tiga bete.</strong><br />
	<em>Kama mimi,</em> yeye pia ana watoto watatu.
</p>
<p>(1) <strong>Hin baytu sen <em>daymo</em> day <em>kom misu to</em>.</strong><br /> Nyumba hii ni kubwa <em>sana</em>
	<em>kama yangu</em>.
</p>
<p>(2) <strong>Hin baytu sen <em>denmo</em> day <em>kom misu to</em>.</strong><br /> Nyumba hii ni kubwa <em>kama
		yangu</em>. </p>
<p>(1) <strong>Mi hare <em>tiga</em> bete <em>kom misu gami</em>.</strong><br /> Nina watoto <em>watatu</em> <em>kama
		mwenzi wangu</em>.</p>
<p>(2) <strong>Mi hare <em>dennumer</em> bete <em>kom misu gami</em>.</strong><br /> Nina watoto <em>wengi kama mwenzi
		wangu</em>.</p>
<p>(1) <strong>Mi le kari <em>dua kilogramo fe</em> risi <em>kom yu</em>.</strong><br /> Nilinunua <em>kilo mbili
		za</em> mchele <em>kama wewe</em>.</p>
<p>(2) <strong>Mi le kari <em>denkwanti</em> risi <em>kom yu</em>.</strong><br /> Nilinunua mchele <em>kiasi kama
		wewe</em>.</p>
<h2>daydenpul</h2>
<p>Neno <strong>daydenpul</strong> ni neno lenye asili lililoundwa na <strong>day-</strong> (<em>awali ya ukuzaji</em>)
	na kiwakilishi kishirikishi <strong>denpul</strong>. Linatafsiriwa kama <em>jinsi gani</em> ikifuatiwa na nomino
	katika mshangao kama yafuatayo:</p>
<p><strong><em>Daydenpul</em> din!</strong><br /> Siku <em>ya namna gani</em>! (au, Siku nzuri ajabu!) </p>
<h2>daydenmo <span id="daydenmmo"></span></h2>
<p>Neno <strong>daydenmo</strong> ni neno lenye asili lililoundwa na <strong>day-</strong> (<em>awali ya ukuzaji</em>)
	na kiwakilishi kishirikishi <strong>denmo</strong>. Ni kielezi cha kiwango kinachomaanisha <em>sana</em>,
	kinapofuatwa na kivumishi/kielezi, au <em>kiasi hicho</em>, kinapofuatwa na nomino iliyorekebishwa. </p>
<p><strong>Yu <em>daydenmo</em> bala.</strong><br /> Wewe una nguvu <em>sana</em>.</p>
<p><strong>Yu hare <em>daydenmo</em> day oko.</strong><br /> Una macho makubwa <em>kiasi hicho</em>.</p>
<p>Neno <strong>daydenmo</strong> pia linatumika kama <strong>daydenpul</strong>. Linamaanisha <em>jinsi gani</em>,
	linapofuatwa na kivumishi/kielezi, au <em>jinsi gani</em>, kinapofuatwa na nomino iliyorekebishwa.</p>
<p><strong><em>Daydenmo</em> meli!</strong><br />
	<em>Jinsi</em> ilivyo nzuri!
</p>
<p><strong><em>Daydenmo</em> meli din!</strong><br /> Siku nzuri <em>ajabu</em>! </p>
<h2>daydenkwanti, daydennumer, denmo multi</h2>
<p>Vile vile, maneno <strong>daydenkwanti</strong> na <strong>daydennumer</strong> yanaweza kutumika kuelezea <em>kiasi
		hicho</em> na <em>wengi kiasi hicho</em>, mtawalia. Vinginevyo, usemi <strong>denmo multi</strong> unaweza
	kutumika kuelezea yoyote kati ya hayo, kwani ni sawa na <strong>daydenkwanti</strong> na
	<strong>daydennumer</strong>.</p>
<h2>moyun</h2>
<p>Neno <strong>moyun</strong> ni neno lenye asili lililoundwa na neno la kiwakilishi kishirikishi <strong>moy</strong>
	na <strong>un</strong> (<em>moja</em>). Linamaanisha <em>kila (mmoja)</em> na linatumika inapobidi kulitofautisha na
	<em>kila/zote</em>.</p>
<p>Linganisha sentensi zifuatazo:</p>
<p><strong>Mi le kari tiga yuxitul cel <em>moyun nini</em>.</strong><br /> Nilinunua vichezeo vitatu kwa ajili ya
	<em>kila mtoto</em>. </p>
<p><strong>Mi le kari tiga yuxitul cel <em>moy nini</em>.</strong><br /> Nilinunua vichezeo vitatu kwa ajili ya
	<em>watoto wote</em>.</p>
